About Me

Born and raised in Indiana, where I also went to college at (Wabash College). At Wabash I majored in Biology and minored in Educational Studies. Along with my studies, I was a part of Delta Tau Delta, the senate's environmental concerns committee, the swim team, and other clubs like the german club, and the Wabash Acts Responsibly club. My four years at Wabash resulted in many acheivements like getting two publications within the environmental field, being awarded the Elliot Churchill award for the most outstanding Biology major in the Junior Class, and finding my way on to the Dean's List almost every semester. Outside of school, I travel a lot. I have been all the way around the world, and have been to almost all continents. Besides traveling, I constantly workout, go on runs, go hiking, swim, or go and do new things.
